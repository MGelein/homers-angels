Wie zijn Homer's Angels

Homer’s Angels is a subteam of the Homer Multitext Project based in Leiden. Under supervision of prof. dr. Ineke Sluiter and dr. Tazuko van Berkel a group of enthusiastic students gather to decipher and digitalize ancient manuscripts and their scholia (short commentaries in the margins of the page). At the moment the Homer Multitext Project focuses on digitalizing the Venetus A manuscript, but after years of hard and dedicated work the digital edition of this manuscript is nearing completion. This year Homer’s Angels will therefore begin working on the Leiden manuscript of the Iliad.

Wat is het Homer Multitext project?

The Homer Multitext Project (HMT) is a project that seeks to present the Homeric Iliad and Odyssey in a critical framework that accounts for the fact that these poems were composed orally over the course of millennia by countless different singers who composed their song during their performance. The project is aiming to create a diplomatic edition of the Venetus A, that will account for the complexity in an oral tradition.
