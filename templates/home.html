What is the Homer Multitext Project?

The Homer Multitext Project (HMT) is a project that seeks to present the Homeric Iliad and Odyssey in a critical framework that follows the theory that these poems were composed orally over the course of millennia by countless different singers who composed their song during their performance. The project aims to create a diplomatic edition of the Venetus A, that will account for the complexity of an oral tradition.
